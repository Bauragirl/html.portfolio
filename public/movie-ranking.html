The Best Movies According to Laura

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Rocky Horror Picture Show

I mean its a cult classic, and for its time it really pushed the boundries of what is and should be socially acceptable. Also, seeing it live with audience participation is where it's at.

Edward Scissor Hands

This one was a favorite of mine growing up so I've picked it for nostalgia reasons really. But Johnny Depp and Winona Ryder, need I say more?

A man called Otto

This is a more recent film I saw on Netflix. It just has everything you need to feel that tug on your heartstrings and just really get you thinking, it's time to live.
